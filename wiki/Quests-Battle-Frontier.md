# Quests: Battle Frontier

The post-Champion sandbox: eight facilities, each with two challengers and a **Frontier Brain** at
level 100. It's the mastery grind of the level-85→100 window — decompression after the story climax,
and where you sharpen a team for the last fight.

> This page is the **roster** (who / where / gated on what). For each hall's **gimmick, stakes, and
> the wager model** — how the Tower's climb, the Castle's ledger, the Cave's descent, and the rest
> actually play — see **[[Guidebook Battle Frontier]]**.

> [!CAUTION]
> **Endgame — post-Royal-League.** The Frontier only opens to a **Champion**. Everything here fights
> at **level 90–100**; treat it as the deep end.

> [!NOTE]
> **Content status (0.5.0):** all eight facilities (challengers + Brains) are cast, placed, and
> gated on the Champion title. The pass/registration economy and the per-facility lore are still in
> authoring. **Hardcore note:** these are your **real** party against level-100 teams — Nuzlocke
> stakes apply. Any "house rules / rental team" flavor is *aspirational*; do not risk a Pokémon you
> can't afford to lose until the exhibition-safety pass lands.

---

## How it works

Each facility is a small ladder: beat **both challengers** (~Lv 90) to open the **Brain** (Lv 100).
Clear a Brain and its hall is yours. All eight are gated on **royal_league_champion** — you cannot
stumble into a level-100 fight before you've earned the throne.

## The eight Brains

| Facility | Frontier Brain | Signature team |
|----------|----------------|----------------|
| **Battle Tower** | Tower Tycoon **Palmer** | Dragonite, Milotic, Rhyperior *(the Platinum homage)* |
| **Battle Castle** | Castle Lord **Percival** | Empoleon, Slowbro, Dusknoir, Steelix |
| **Battle Factory** | Factory Head **Noland** | Magnezone, Scizor, Metagross, Porygon-Z |
| **Battle Arcade** | Arcade Star **Dahlia** | Blaziken, Togekiss, Garchomp, Zoroark |
| **Deep Dark Cave** | Cave Warden **Selene** | Mismagius, Sableye, Gengar, Hydreigon, Tyranitar, **Darkrai** |
| **Battle Pyramid** | Pyramid King **Brandon** | Regirock, Registeel, Regice, **Regigigas** |
| **Battle Market** | Market Mogul **Sterling** | Zoroark, Chansey, Alakazam, Porygon-Z |
| **Battle Port** | Port Admiral **Horatio** | Pelipper, Gyarados, Kingdra, **Lugia** |

Every Brain fields a level-100 team; several carry a signature legendary (Darkrai, Regigigas, Lugia)
as their prestige piece. The two challengers per facility are the warm-up — beat them first.

## Where it sits

The Frontier is the **"after."** It depends on nothing downstream and gates nothing — pure long-tail
mastery content for the training window before (and after) the final boss. For the endgame story
beats it sits inside, see **[[Guidebook Act III]]**. Other endgame content in the same window: the
**[[Guidebook Nobles]]** and the post-league shrines (**[[Guidebook Shrines]]**).
