### The gimmick — the Gauntlet + a 2-vs-1 finish

Deepcore is run as a **marshal-led pit**: you fight down the ladder to **Gauntlet Marshal Osei**,
and the leader beat ends in a **2-vs-1 double** — Bruno's pair against you *and* an AI sparring
partner fighting at your side. Play it as a doubles match, not a solo. As always, softening the
rank-and-file drains the leader's team.

## Side Quests

> [!NOTE]
> **Shipped.** Deepcore's four town side-quests are compiled, placed, and tracked. Amounts are face value; quest payouts pay the **Verified Rate** (a haircut that widens as the instability index climbs), and by Deepcore that haircut is *visible* — the receipts read short on purpose.

Deepcore is the town where the money stops pretending. The nurse at the pit-head Center, **Nurse Rurik**, doubles as the rumour hub — heal at the posted rate and she points you at whatever's going. Everything below hangs on the town's two moods: a **blue-collar grievance** in a dojo town of miners-turned-fighters, and a **cover-up** the Company would rather you didn't read.

### Deep Restructuring — ✅ Done

**Foreman Kang** (**@ 1120 111 3300**, by the pit-head) is a retired union pit-foreman the Company "deep-restructured" onto a stool by the pit-head — and he can smell fraud in the mine's re-verified ore ledger even if he can't read the doctored columns. He sends you to scrounge **6 struck signature pages** the Company shredded around the field office; hand them back and Kang reads the robbery out loud — the nether-star vein halved on paper and re-signed under a stranger's name. Turn-in pays **900 CD** plus a **Black Belt** and a training pack. Afterward he offers an **optional, decline-able wager**: 250 CD against the officer guarding the vault — win and the stake doubles back. The officer's team sits under your cap, and he greets you the way Company staff do now — *you're supposed to be a closed file.*

### The Iron Ladder — ✅ Done

**Old Dun** (**@ 1060 119 3200**, at the played-out shaft) worked a shaft until it ran dry and now runs a knock-down gauntlet down the played-out hole: **three fights, back to back, no heals between rounds** — pure brutal-Nuzlocke spice, your party carrying every bruise into the next fight. Fully **opt-in** and gated behind proving yourself in the gym first. Losing a round just ends the run — no fee, and you can step off any time. Clear all three (Lv 34 / 35 / 37) for **700 CD** and an **Expert Belt**, one-time.

### The Better Rate — ✅ Done

**Corliss** (**@ 1180 123 3260**, at the mine commissary) is a grain buyer working the mine commissary, pitching the Company's wheat-backed scrip to miners nervous about their short pay. There's **no cash reward and no forced fight** — the quest *is* the recognition beat. Once you've **freed two fields**, Corliss recognizes you mid-trade and turns wary — *a face from the old company, probably nothing* — but still trades. The same two-fields threshold also eases Deepcore's shop prices, so this is where your clawed-back fields visibly help the currency *and* where the cover-up first eyes you.

### The Deep Office — ✅ Done

Interact with the sealed branch office **@ 985 114 3120**. Deep in a flooded, forgotten wing of the mine — sealed off before anyone thought to scrub it — sits an **abandoned Company branch office**, pre-coup and untouched. On the wall hangs an intact portrait the rest of the region's walls no longer carry; the brass nameplate beneath it has been gouged to bare metal by someone in a hurry, long ago. No battle, no cash: just a face that holds your eye longer than a stranger's should, and a stir you can't explain. The desk still holds an unshredded **shipping manifest** routing the mine's reserves down to **Gaviota Port**. The tracker points you here only once the restructuring quest is underway.

## Where it sits on the road

Deepcore is where **Act II opens in your wallet** — the instability index climbs toward 32, quest
payouts take their first visible haircut, and wheat-traders recognize you once you've freed a couple
of fields. The field-liberation campaign that gates the HQ raid is explained on the
**[[Guidebook Route Map]]** and (rules) the Wheat War hub. Next stop: **Gaviota Port** (Water, gym 5).
